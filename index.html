Directions

I have a new thought on this second web page. My goal is to make it so people who I am connected with can post articles and discussions on this page. The idea would be like a reddit hosted by myself. It is more important to me that the contributors were born in the 80's and Iowa has influenced their life in some substantial way. The proximity to myself is less critical

To that end, I will need to set this webpage up so people can request a login. That login will need to go through an approval process. Once approved, people can post on any topic they choose. They can also create their own topics

This brings me to the next things I will need to create, a beginning list of topics along with the ability to create additional topics. Should be a fun endeavor and expand my skills!
